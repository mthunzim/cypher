We provide total Business Solutions

Cypher Technology is a company that focuses on design, deployment of networking infrastructure, databases, surveillance systems, audio systems, alarm systems and OSS/BSS systems. Our expertise in the development of efficient, scalable infrastructure, architecture and supporting systems enables our customers to optimize costs in their environment while providing improved systems and network availability, performance and good customer experience. Cypher Technology strives to offer complete infrastructure solutions or enhancements that enable businesses and service providers to remain profitable.


Our view of a complete infrastructure extends considerably beyond the equipment and the network and includes the following:

  • Premium services and beyond your expectation
  • Get the best support among all venders
  • Great Price Competitive
  • Service creation and definition and Information of service performance
  • Activation and provisioning of services to customers
  • Data and systems migration during upgrades and new implementations
  • Ongoing maintenance and sustainability of the service to customers
  • Design of physical facilities including datacenters and cable plants
  • Perform systems, network and datacenter audits

For Us to be able to achieve these goals, the company believes in multi-vendor solutions and will support vendors that provide the best technological solutions on specific functional areas. Our highly experienced team has vast background in Networking (IP/MPLS), Databases (MySQL, Oracle MSSQL & Informix), Operating Systems (Windows & Linux) and Service Provider Infrastructure (Cisco, Juniper and Ericsson) in Swaziland and we are planning to spread our services across in the region and beyond.


This experience covers all aspects from the physical fiber plant in the ground, through to complex network protocols, right up to the development of the services deliverd to customers. We produce high availability and business cintinuity by designing and implementing backup solutions, as well as disaster recovery planning. In sum, Cypher Technologies is an innovative Information Communication Technology (ICT) solutions consultancy firm. We offer services aimed towards helping institutions/businesses to deliver on their strategic and operational objectives through the use of proven project management methodology, robus technologies and highly skilled, experienced and committed staff.
